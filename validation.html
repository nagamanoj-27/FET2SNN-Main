🔬 Model Calibration & Validation Summary
This suite compares FET2SNN predictions against rigorous reference Synopsys Sentaurus TCAD simulations (utilizing Lombardi mobility and MLDA quantum corrections) and peer-reviewed literature benchmarks. The validation dataset spans 17 distinct GAA nanosheet and FinFET geometries across 5nm to 45nm nodes. Custom datasets can be uploaded live in CSV format.
