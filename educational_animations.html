🧬 Biological Neural Network

🐢 🐇
Action Potential Electric impulses (glowing particles) travel along the pre‑synaptic axon, cross the synaptic cleft via neurotransmitters, and excite the post‑synaptic dendrite. This spike‑based communication inspired spiking neural networks (SNNs).

⚡ Transistor → Neural Hardware

🐢 🐇
GAA Transistor Charge carriers (electrons) flow through the nanosheet gate stack (input weight). They accumulate in the LIF neuron block – when threshold is reached, a spike (output) is generated. This mimics a biological synapse.
